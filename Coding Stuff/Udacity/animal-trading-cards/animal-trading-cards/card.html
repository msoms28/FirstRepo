Labrador Retriever

In 1917 the Labrador Retriever was officially recognized by the American Kennel Club.

  • Scientific Name: Labrador Retriever - Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height: 22.5 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years
  • Origination: Newfoundland and Labrador

Originally the St. John's Water Dog, this breed was created when small water dogs were bred with Newfoundlands. Their coats can be yellow, black, or chocolate.
